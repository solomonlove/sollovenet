Transforming Manufacturing Development in Ethiopia

The Manufacturing Industry Development Institute (MIDI) is implementing an advanced Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system to revolutionize its operations and enhance support for the nation's manufacturing sector.

Discover the Initiative

About The Manufacturing Industry Development Institute (MIDI)

Established to be a cornerstone for industrial advancement, MIDI is dedicated to enhancing the competitiveness and sustainable growth of Ethiopia's manufacturing sector. The institute provides a comprehensive range of support initiatives, including research and development, product and technology development, market analysis, and specialized workforce training. MIDI serves as a vital catalyst for innovation and efficiency, fostering collaboration between industry professionals and researchers to develop tailored solutions that meet specific industry needs and address evolving challenges.

The ERP Initiative: A New Era of Efficiency

MIDI is embarking on the implementation of an integrated ERP system to streamline its diverse functions and services. This strategic move aims to overcome challenges posed by fragmented systems and manual processes, leading to improved data management, streamlined workflows, and enhanced collaboration across all departments and research centers. The ERP will serve as a central platform, empowering MIDI to provide more effective and timely support to manufacturing firms, fostering innovation and sustainable growth throughout the industry.

Our Implementation Journey & Timeline

The ERP implementation is a phased approach. The following are indicative durations for each key phase, based on the project plan (refer to Table 2 of the proposal for full details).

1. Project Initiation & Planning

Scope definition, team assembly, detailed roadmap, risk assessment.

Estimated Duration: 4 Weeks

2. Requirements Gathering & Analysis

Stakeholder interviews, process mapping, functional needs, gap analysis.

Estimated Duration: 6 Weeks

3. System Design & Configuration

ERP architecture design, module customization, integration planning.

Estimated Duration: 10 Weeks

4. Data Migration

Data cleansing, mapping, migration execution, and validation.

Estimated Duration: 6 Weeks (may overlap)

5. Testing & Quality Assurance

Unit, integration, User Acceptance (UAT), performance, security testing.

Estimated Duration: 8 Weeks

6. Training & Change Management

Material development, training sessions, super-user program.

Estimated Duration: 5 Weeks (intensive pre-go-live)

7. Go-Live & Deployment

Final migration, system deployment, intensive go-live support.

Estimated Duration: 2 Weeks (cutover & initial support)

8. Post-Implementation Support & Continuous Improvement

Ongoing technical support, maintenance, user feedback, enhancements.

Status: Ongoing

Note: These durations are illustrative. The comprehensive project schedule is detailed in the official ERP Proposal document.
